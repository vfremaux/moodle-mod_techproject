setMonthNames(names)

  • names - {array of string}, default is [“January”, “February”, “March”, “April”, “May”, “June”, “July”, “August”, “September”, “October”, “November”, “December”]

defines full month names for your locale. It is then used in month axis labels. 

    ganttChartControl.useShortMonthNames(false);
    ganttChartControl.setMonthNames(["Январь","Февраль","Март","Апрель","Май","Июнь","Июль","Август","Сентябрь","Октябрь","Ноябрь","Декабрь"]);
